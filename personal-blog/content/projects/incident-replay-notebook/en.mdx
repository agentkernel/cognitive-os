<h2 id="problem">Problem</h2>

An ordinary timeline can easily collapse “request dispatched,” “receipt missing,” and “operation did not happen” into one event.

<h2 id="constraints">Constraints</h2>

Replay must preserve the original Intent and idempotency binding. Any compensation needs a new Intent and independent authorization.

<h2 id="approach">Approach</h2>

Reorder the record around fixed prior state, dispatch, observation gap, reconciliation, verification, and disposition, sending any unclosed branch to quarantine.

<h2 id="outcome">Outcome</h2>

The sample template forces an author to label unknown facts and evidence sources. It does not describe a real incident.

<h2 id="reflection">Reflection</h2>

The most important quality of an incident document is not a complete story. It is refusing to fill an evidence gap with narrative.
