<h2 id="problem">Problem</h2>

“Retrieve the most relevant content” does not say what may be read, which evidence is required, or what a compression step loses.

<h2 id="constraints">Constraints</h2>

The prototype must separate hard governance gates from replaceable ranking policy and avoid presenting a ContextView as a trusted database.

<h2 id="approach">Approach</h2>

Show admission, pre-filtering, candidates, per-object authorization, budgeting, and loss declarations in order, then label the output as a non-authority projection.

<h2 id="outcome">Outcome</h2>

The sample helps a reviewer locate fail-closed and degradable paths, but includes no runtime, real data, or conformance evidence.

<h2 id="reflection">Reflection</h2>

Putting relevance after governance removes one seductive score from the interface and creates several boundaries worth questioning.
